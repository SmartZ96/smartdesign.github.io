RESEARCH PROJECT

For my honour's project at university I decided to create a short video using various enhancements for audiences that may have any forms of impairment such as audible and visual, the tools used to heavily enhance the experience was the audio narration in th background for the visually impaired while the subtitles on the screen is for the audibly impaired.
